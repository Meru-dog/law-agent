PAYMENT AGREEMENT

This Payment Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into as of January 15, 2026, between:

PARTIES:
- Acme Corporation, a Delaware corporation ("Buyer")
- Widget Industries Inc., a California corporation ("Seller")

TERMS AND CONDITIONS:

1. PAYMENT TERMS
The Payment Term shall be thirty (30) days from the date of invoice. All payments shall be made in U.S. Dollars.

2. LATE PAYMENT FEES
Late fees of one and one-half percent (1.5%) per month shall apply to any overdue payments. Such fees shall accrue from the due date until payment is received in full.

3. DELIVERY SCHEDULE
Seller shall deliver goods within fifteen (15) business days of receiving a purchase order from Buyer.

4. WARRANTY
Seller warrants that all goods delivered shall be free from defects in materials and workmanship for a period of one (1) year from the date of delivery.

5. TERMINATION
Either party may terminate this Agreement with thirty (30) days written notice to the other party. Termination shall not affect any obligations incurred prior to the effective date of termination.

6. GOVERNING LAW
This Agreement shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of the State of California, without regard to its conflict of law provisions.

7. DISPUTE RESOLUTION
Any disputes arising under this Agreement shall be resolved through binding arbitration in San Francisco, California.

8. CONFIDENTIALITY
Both parties agree to maintain the confidentiality of any proprietary information shared during the term of this Agreement.
